Starry Night

Vincent Van Gogh’s,The Starry Night was created by him at the asylum in Saint-Rémy.represent his turbulent mental state, with the swirling sky and dark, flame-like cypress tree symbolizing his inner turmoil and a connection to death, while the bright stars offer a glimmer of hope and a longing for dreams and the divine.

The last supper

The Last Supper is a High Renaissance mural painting by Leonardo da Vinci (1495–1498) located in the refectory of the Convent of Santa Maria delle Grazie in Milan, Italy. It depicts the moment Jesus announces his impending betrayal by one of his apostles, capturing the apostles' shock and despair.

The Scream

The painting is seen as an iconic representation of angst and the negative human emotions. The main figure of a painting is a human being, with a bald head, pale face, mouth open in a silent scream, and hands on the ears as if not wanting to listen to its own voice.
